## Beneath the Euphoria: A Sober Look at Valuation

The current market exuberance, with the S&P 500 breaching 7,000 and the KOSPI eyeing 6,000, is undoubtedly exciting for many. However, a measured perspective, rooted in fundamental analysis, compels us to question the sustainability of this rally. While technological innovation is indeed transformative, the speed and scale of recent gains in AI-adjacent stocks often outpace any conceivable earnings growth or tangible value creation. The narrative of "recalibration" advanced by some masks what appears to be classic irrational exuberance, where price becomes detached from intrinsic worth. We must ask: are these valuations supported by current and projected cash flows, or merely by speculative fervor?
